# Contributing to GRBToolkit

Thank you for your interest in improving GRBToolkit.

At this time, active development is internal, but:
- Bug reports are welcome
- Suggestions or validation via Issues are appreciated
- Pull requests may be considered for modular tools or documentation

All contributions should:
- Respect scientific reproducibility and clarity
- Avoid feature creep
- Maintain simplicity, symbolic clarity, and functional elegance

By contributing, you agree to license your work under the same MIT license as this repository.
